Tripartite Cash Transfer Agreement
This Tripartite Cash Transfer Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between the Client, BDSec Securities LLC ("Broker"), and the Mongolian Central Securities Depository ("MCSD"). 1. Purpose This Agreement enables the Client to transfer cash electronically between their designated bank account, their Nominal (brokerage) account held with the Broker, and their account at the MCSD. 2. Designated Bank Account The Client shall designate one (1) bank account to be used for all transfers under this Agreement. The Client may change the designated bank account at any time by submitting a request through the trading platform. 3. Transfers Funds may be transferred from the designated bank account to the Nominal account for the purpose of securities trading, and from the Nominal account back to the designated bank account upon withdrawal request. 4. Authorization By signing this Agreement, the Client authorizes the Broker and the MCSD to process transfer instructions issued through the trading platform without requiring a separate physical signature for each transaction. 5. Liability The Client is responsible for ensuring that the designated bank account remains active and accurately registered under the Client's name. The Broker is not liable for delays caused by incorrect account information. 6. Termination This Agreement remains in effect until the Client closes their trading account or requests termination in writing. By signing below, the Client acknowledges they have read, understood, and agree to the terms above.
